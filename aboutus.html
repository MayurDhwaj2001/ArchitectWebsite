About Us

Company History

2005: ARCHItects founded in New Delhi with a team of 4 architects.
2010: Expanded to international projects in Dubai and Singapore.
2017: Won the Global Green Architecture Award.
2023: Opened our 5th design studio in London.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To design spaces that inspire and elevate lives, combining innovation with sustainability.

Vision: To be a global leader in architecture and design, creating landmarks that shape the future of urban living.
